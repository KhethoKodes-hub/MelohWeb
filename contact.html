Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers to Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Northern Cape region with our offices in Pofadder and Kuruman. However, we can accommodate projects throughout South Africa depending on the scope and requirements.

How quickly can you start a project?

After initial consultation and agreement on project scope, we can typically mobilize within 1-2 weeks for most projects. Emergency maintenance services can often be deployed within 24-48 hours.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide detailed, no-obligation estimates for all our services. Contact us with your project details, and we'll prepare a comprehensive quote.

What safety standards do you follow?

We are committed to zero harm and adhere to strict Health, Safety, Environment, and Quality (HSEQ) standards. Our management system is consistent with international standards and South African legislation.

Are you available for emergency services?

Yes, we offer emergency maintenance and repair services. Contact us directly at 067 227 5625 for urgent matters outside of regular business hours.
