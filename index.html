Who We Are

Driven by Purpose, Defined by Excellence

Our Story

Established in 2024, Meloh Holdings Pty Ltd represents a new generation of South African enterprise. As a sister company of Mantomba Holdings, we're a 100% black-owned, women and youth-led organization with offices in Pofadder and Kuruman, Northern Cape.

We believe that real and significant change to the South African economy comes through the efforts of individuals from diverse cultural and economic backgrounds who remain committed to creating innovative synergies, forging new partnerships, and promoting social cohesion.

Our team comprises phenomenal women and youth—dedicated, passionate professionals using their knowledge and experience to make a real difference in the lives of South Africa's previously disenfranchised communities.

Our Vision

To support human progress through an integrative balance of individual, group, and community development—seen as synergistic rather than antagonistic factors of human life and societal advancement. We strive to be the leading provider in our domain.

Our Mission

To be the first helping-hand in your time of need. We are committed to offering service that is fast, effective, and reliable. Customer satisfaction is our priority! We empower our recipients by utilizing our skills and maximizing our resources.

Our Core Values

The Principles That Guide Everything We Do

Respect

One of our most important traits because it creates a positive work culture for colleagues, clients, and partners to accomplish goals together. A respectful attitude is our priority, regardless of personal feelings.

Integrity

We demonstrate honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all actions and decisions. Our word is our bond, and trust is the foundation of every relationship we build.

Safety & Well-being

Committed to Health, Safety & Environment excellence. HSEQ is integral to how we do business. Our goal is zero harm to people, environment, and property at all times.

Education & Growth

We educate and skill our team members by giving them opportunities to register, study, and train, empowering them to uplift the company they've found a home in.

Excellence

Optimum attention to every detail of the job at hand for all our clients. We don't just meet standards—we set them.

Community Impact

Dedicated to women and youth empowerment, creating lasting change in South Africa's previously disenfranchised communities through economic participation.

Health & Safety

Our Commitment to Zero Harm

Health, Safety & Environment Statement

Meloh Holdings Pty Ltd is committed to Health, Safety & Environment and quality service. HSEQ is integral to the way we do business. We recognize communities, employees, business partners, suppliers and customers as stakeholders. We are committed to continual improvement in our performance.

We commit to zero harm to people, environment and property, efficient and total customer satisfaction and improved value for everyone. Our management system is consistent with international and organizational standards, legislation and other relevant requirements.

We undertake to ensure that our management system is developed, documented, implemented and maintained in a manner that is both understood and effective at all levels in our business, to enable us to:

  • Identify, assess and manage risks to our employees, clients' employees and the environment
  • Commit to achieving leading industry practice and recognition
  • Meet and where appropriate, exceed applicable legislation
  • Lead and develop our people and provide resources to achieve our targets
  • Support the fundamental human rights of employees and safeguard them from exposure to unacceptable risk

We will seek opportunities to share our success by developing partnerships that focus on creating sustainable value for all stakeholders. We will regularly review our progress and communicate results to all stakeholders in order to enable a relationship based on honesty, openness and mutual trust.

We will be successful when we achieve our targets toward our goal of zero harm and are valued by the clients in which we work.
